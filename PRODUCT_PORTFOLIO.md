## Fintech & financial intelligence

**Wealth Command** *(live: wealth-command.com)*
An AI-powered wealth and tax intelligence platform: a full-picture engine that synthesizes a user's financial data into prioritized, cited recommendations, AI tax analysis, document extraction to tax forms, a deterministic budget engine, and planning modules (annuities, life insurance, self-directed Roth, entity architecture, audit response). Encrypted, advisor-grade multi-client.

**Supply Cost Analyzer**
An interactive "Supply Chaos Score" diagnostic that estimates hidden costs from fragmented vendors, stockouts, and manual ordering — a lead-generation calculator with real cost modeling.

A SaaS platform for managing multiple healthcare-adjacent brands: centralized brand management, AI content generation, creative asset organization, scheduling, analytics, and lead management, with short-form video workflows and chatbot-to-human handoffs (Publer, Metricool, Canva).
